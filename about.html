At Coding Clubhouse, we're reimagining access to tech education. We're a non-profit committed to giving every child — especially those in underrepresented communities — the opportunity to explore, learn and create with technology.

We do this through Dream Beyond Limits, teacher training, summer coding classes and our Code Equity curriculum.

Our Mission & Vision

We're teachers, technologists, creators, and dreamers — united by a shared belief that every child deserves a chance to thrive through technology.

Our Mission

To make computer science education accessible to all children by equipping them and their communities with skills, confidence, and creativity to thrive in a digital world.

Our Vision

A future where every child, regardless of background, can learn, innovate, and lead through technology.
